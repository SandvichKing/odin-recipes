"Burnt" Basque Cheese Cake

Description

Enjoy the rich goodness of Basque cheesecake - a dessert that's both creamy and indulgent.

Picture a caramelized exterior giving way to a luscious, velvety inside. For a fruity kick, add sweet blueberries on top, or go for ultimate luxury with a dollop of whipped cream.

Whether you savor it plain or jazz it up with toppings, this cheesecake is a simple yet heavenly treat that promises to satisfy your sweet cravings.

Ingredients

  • 3 Packages Cream Cheese
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 cup Thickened Cream
  • 1 cup Granulated Sugar
  • 3 Tblsp Cornflour
  • 1/2 Tsp salt
  • 1/2 Vanilla Extract
  • Blueberries (optional)
  • Whipped Cream (optional)

Steps

  1. Set aside cream cheese, eggs and thickened cream until it's at room temperature.
  2. Preheat oven to 200 degrees celcius
  3. Butter cake pan. Cut a sheet of parchment paper large enough to line inside of pan. Flatten out any major creases and cut out excess paper.
  4. Combine cream cheese, sugar, flour, salt in a bowl. Mix with a spatula until very smooth and creamy consistency.
  5. Add vanilla extract and 1 egg, whisk to combine. Add each egg at a time and continue whisking. Pour thickened cream and mix until smooth.
  6. Pour batter into cake pan. Tap pan against counter to remove any excess air.
  7. Bake in preheated oven until puffed, well browned and almost burnt at edges (approximately 1 hour).
  8. Let cheesecake cool to room temperature (about 30 minutes). Place in fridge and leave overnight
  9. Top cheesecake with blueberries and whipped cream then enjoy!
