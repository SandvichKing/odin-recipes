"Burnt" Basque Cheese Cake

Description

A homemade cheesecake made with simple ingredients, topped with blueberry jam and whipped cream on the side

Ingredients

  • 3 Packages Cream Cheese
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 cup Thickened Cream
  • 1 cup Granulated Sugar
  • 3 Tblsp Cornflour
  • 1/2 Tsp salt
  • 1/2 Vanilla Extract
  • Blueberries
  • Whipped Cream

Steps

  1. Set aside cream cheese, eggs and thickened cream until it's at room temperature.
  2. Preheat oven to 200 celcius
  3. Butter a cake pan. Cut a sheet of parchment paper large enough to line inside of pan. Flatten out any major creases and cut out excess paper.
  4. Combine cream cheese, sugar, flour, and salt in a bowl. Mix with a spatula until very smooth and creamy consistency.
  5. Add vanilla extract and 1 egg, whisk to combine. Add each egg at a time and continue whisking. Pour heavy cream and mix until smooth.
  6. Pour batter into cake pan. Tap pan against counter to remove any excess air.
  7. Bake in preheated oven until puffed, well browned and almost burnt at edges (approximately 1 hour).
  8. Let cheesecake cool to room temperature (about 30 minutes). Place in fridge and leave overnight
  9. Top cheesecake with blueberries and whipped cream then enjoy!
