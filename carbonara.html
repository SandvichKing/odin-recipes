Carbonara

Description

Savor the delicious blend of flavors in this exquisite carbonara, where smoky bacon, earthy mushrooms, and tender chicken come together in perfect harmony.

Boasting a creamy texture and a symphony of tastes, the crispy bacon adds a satisfying crunch, the mushrooms bring umami richness, and the chicken contributes a hearty protein element.

This carbonara showcases the beauty of combining simple yet high-quality ingredients for a memorable Italian-inspired dining experience.

Ingredients

  • 100g Pecorino cheese block (no peppercorns)
  • 500g Bacon
  • 500g Chicken (thighs/breasts)
  • 200g Mushrooms
  • 3 Eggs + 1 egg yolk
  • 4 Garlic Cloves

Steps

  1. Grate the cheese and mix with eggs
  2. Cook chicken until brown and put aside
  3. Cook bacon until crispy, add mushrooms and garlic, put aside.
  4. Cook pasta until al dente, drain, then put aside.
  5. Mix the cheese and egg mixture with the pasta and stir vigorously.
  6. Add bacon, mushroom, garlic and chicken to pasta mixture.
  7. Season to taste with salt and pepper, and enjoy!
