Kimchi Fried Rice

Description

Kimchi Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 300g Kimchi
  • 4 Cups White Rice
  • 1 Large Red Onion
  • 1 Large Carrot
  • 1 Large Zucchini
  • 1 Tblsp Unsalted Butter
  • 1-2 Tblsp Gochujang
  • 2 Tblsp Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Dice zucchini, red onion and kimchi into small chunks and set aside
  2. Shred Carrots into grater and set aside
  3. Melt unsalted butter into pan, add vegetables and stirfry
  4. Add rice, Gochujang and soy sauce, mix some more
