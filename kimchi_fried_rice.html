Kimchi Fried Rice

Description

From the chewy rice to the crunchy vegetables, this kimchi fried rice creates a satisfying and deeply flavorful mouthfeel. It's a celebration of Korean culinary ingenuity.

The vibrant heat of gochujang, a Korean red chili paste, melds with the bold tanginess of kimchi. The addition of zucchini adds a crisp freshness, while the sweetness of red onion complements the savory ensemble.

Sesame seeds provide a subtle nutty crunch, and dried seaweed garnish adds a hint of oceanic umami, transforming each spoonful into a dynamic medley of textures.

Ingredients

  • 300g Kimchi
  • 4 Cups White Rice
  • 1 Large Red Onion
  • 1 Large Carrot
  • 1 Large Zucchini
  • 1 Tblsp Unsalted Butter
  • 1-2 Tblsp Gochujang
  • 2 Tblsp Soy Sauce
  • Toasted sesame seeds (optional)
  • Dried seaweed (optional)
  • Eggs (optional)

Steps

  1. Dice zucchini, red onion and kimchi into small chunks and set aside
  2. Shred carrots into grater and set aside
  3. Melt unsalted butter into pan, add vegetables and panfry
  4. Add rice, gochujang, soy sauce and mix vigorously
  5. Garnish with toasted sesame seeds, dried seaweed and top with a fried egg then enjoy!
